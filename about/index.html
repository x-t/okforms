okforms

An open platform for forms and polls

Forms

With the simple form creator you can create forms fast for absolutely no cost. Protect your form's answers with a password that's surprisingly not encoded in plain text.

Polls

Create polls instantly with many answer options.

Completely anonymous

You don't need an account to create or vote on a form. In fact, this service does not support any registration.

Open source

The source code and the product are licensed under the MIT (Expat) license, making okforms free (libre) software.

Lightweight

The source code of okforms does not depend on any frameworks, everything was written from scratch with the intent to be fast and compatible. You can vote on forms and polls from even w3m.

Export options

Export your form's answers as XML and JSON formats. Your form's answers will stay even if the form expired.
